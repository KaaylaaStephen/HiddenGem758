ABOUT US




HIDDEN GEM 758 is a private owned small business in Castries, Saint Lucia. The company mainly focuses on women's wear, but it also offers men's apparel, children's clothes, accessories, bathing suits and other fashion items. The busines was founded in 2019.All our clothing items are custom made and machine sewn in order to please our customers. We try our hardest to keep up with fashion trends and ensure that everyone can enjoy the beauty of fashion without having to break a bank.
